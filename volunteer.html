ESLL Volunteer Service Requirement

~ Visit Sign-Up Genius to register for your volunteer shift today! ~

As an all-volunteer organization, East Sacramento Little League requires that every family donate a minimum of three (3) hours of volunteer time to league activities during the baseball/softball season PER PLAYER to assist the league in providing high-quality athletic experiences for our youth.

Numerous volunteer opportunities are provided throughout the season for families to actively fulfill the ESLL Volunteer Requirement including Snack Shack shifts, Committee Opportunities and/or work at League Events such as Opening Day, Hit-A-Thon, Equipment Swap, etc. The most common opportunity to complete your Volunteer Service requirement is work in our Snack Shack.

Information surrounding current volunteer opportunities is given below, and a link to register for specific volunteer shifts through Sign-Up Genius will be made available very soon. Watch your email for details.

ESLL REQUIRES a $200.00 Volunteer Service Deposit PER PLAYER. A check made out to East Sac Little League for the total amount must be received and confirmed by the League BEFORE a player may tryout (Majors, AAA, AA and Majors, Minors and Rookies softball) or receive a uniform (Single A and Tee Ball baseball). Deposits are capped at $400.00 per family for families with more than two children participating in the league.

Upon completion of the total minimum requirement of volunteer hours per player and per family, the original deposit check will be returned to the parent or guardian. All volunteer work must be completed no later than Saturday, June 2nd, 2018, and total hours required for all players in the family must be completed before a deposit check is returned.

Volunteer deposit checks should be made out to East Sacramento Little League and mailed at your earliest convenience to the following address:

East Sacramento Little League

Attn: Volunteer Coordinator

P.O. BOX 19596

Sacramento, CA 95819

Volunteer Service Requirement, Opt-Out Opportunity

If you are aware in advance that you WILL NOT be able to complete the ESLL Volunteer Service Requirement during the 2018 spring season, the league offers the opportunity to pay out your family volunteer service requirement in advance as a one-time donation to East Sacramento Little League.

To opt-out, simply print and complete the Opt-Out Form provided below, and mail with a check made out to East Sacramento Little League for the total deposit to:

Substitutes - Snack Bar

If you are unable to work your Snack Shack shift, substitute individuals may be available whom you may hire to fulfill your Volunteer Service Requirement. All substitutes must be at least 16 years old, be approved by ESLL, and have attended Snack Shack training to be eligible. Substitutes earn $40 per shift. Hiring and payment is arranged between you and the substitute. ESLL will acknowledge the substitute as working your shift and your $200 volunteer deposit check will be returned.
