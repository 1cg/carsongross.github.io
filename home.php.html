Welcome to East Sacramento Little League! Celebrating 67 Years!

East Sacramento Little League (ESLL), founded in 1951, offers positive, instructive, community-focused recreational baseball and softball for children ages 4-12. Our league is an all-volunteer organization, and takes pride in providing opportunities for children in our community and their families to learn and experience America's greatest pastime.
